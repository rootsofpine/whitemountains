December 2023 North Conway

The Morning My Grandfather Showed Me

I was seven when he took me up before dawn. We didn't talk much—that wasn't his way. But when the sun broke over the ridge and hit the frost on the pines, turning everything to gold and diamond, he just stood there. "This," he said, "is why we stay." Thirty years later, I understand.

— Submitted by Sarah M.
